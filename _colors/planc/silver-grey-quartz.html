A Bright Elegance with Silver Grey Quartz

Truly a chameleon of a wall covering, Silver Grey Quartz is equally at place in more classic designs paired with brass and bronze fixtures as well as more modern styling bumping up against stainless and carbon accoutrements. With a silvery grey speckled color, this stone is super consistent from piece to piece allowing for experimentation with the design and layout of the individual strips in each Planc set to create a pattern unique to the project.
