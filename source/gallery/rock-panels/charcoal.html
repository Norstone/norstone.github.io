REFINED GREY LEDGESTONE PANELS

Charcoal Rock Panels ™

A part of our collection of Ledgestone Panels, the Charcoal Rock Panel is our flagship product designed initially to quickly transform plain landscaping walls into textured, natural stacked stone walls. Due in part to its light weight and to it the straight-forward installation if offers, it didn't take long for this product to become a favorite interior wall finish among renowned interior designers. Used consistently on comemrcial and residential interiors, the Charcoal Rock Panel is fantastic choice where cool, textured grey tones are desired.

 

Not One Detail Overlooked.       

Interlocking Finger Joints are the only proven method for creating a truly seamless finish along the corner. Norstone's patent-pending Fingerjoint Corner System, which is available for all our Wall Panel Products, consists of two smaller panels which join together at the corner to create the perfect corner treatment. Each pair is built together at the same time. This ensure a seamless fit every time.
